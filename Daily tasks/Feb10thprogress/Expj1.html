Virat Kohli - A Living Legend

Virat Kohli, born on November 5, 1988, is an iconic cricketer and one of the greatest batsmen of modern cricket. Known for his aggressive style, unmatched consistency, and dedication to the game, Kohli has become a role model for countless cricket fans worldwide.

Achievements

  • Former Captain of the Indian Cricket Team in all formats.
  • Ranked among the top batsmen in the ICC rankings for years.
  • Fastest player to 10,000 runs in ODIs.
  • Multiple-time recipient of ICC Player of the Year.

Why I Admire Him

Virat's determination, discipline, and passion for cricket are incredibly inspiring. His ability to lead and perform under pressure sets him apart as one of the best. Beyond cricket, his commitment to fitness and charitable efforts make him a true role model.
